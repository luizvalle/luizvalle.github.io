About me

Bio

I am a third-year Computer Science major and Applied Mathematics minor at the Columbia University School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS). I am specializing in Intelligent Systems (machine learning, data science, robotics, and systems capable of exhibiting "human-like" intelligence) with electives in Software Systems (operating systems, networks, and databases). I am passionate about using mathematics and computing to make sense of data and draw interesting conclusions. I also enjoy learning how things work at a fundamental level, which is why I am studying the low-level details of how computers work.
